Create Professional Looking Buttons with CSS3

2011 February 9 at Boston PHP

From Boston PHP: Create professional looking buttons with CSS3. Join Jen Strickland as she shows you great solutions to make buttons and links look and feel very professional - all with CSS. Whatever you do, don't waste time creating images as buttons and links. These solutions use CSS and allow you to easily implement design and terminology changes on your sites. It's not too hard to do, but once implemented, you will love the results. Jen will show and demonstrate most of these solutions and then show you some of the better ones with live working code so everyone can follow along.

  • Review and demo of leading CSS button solutions
  • Live coding example adding CSS buttons to a site
  • Discussion and your questions
